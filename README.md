# Restaurant Data Analysis Internship

This repository contains the work completed during my internship at **Cognifyz**, focusing on data analysis and insights derived from restaurant datasets. The project involved cleaning, analyzing, and visualizing data to extract meaningful information about cuisines, cities, price ranges, delivery options, and more.

---

## **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**

- Identified the **top cuisines** and their representation across the dataset.
- Analyzed **city-level trends**, highlighting areas with the most restaurants and best ratings.
- Explored **price range distribution**, providing insights into pricing trends and their impact on services.
- Examined the relationship between **online delivery and restaurant ratings**.
- Investigated **popular cuisine combinations** and their correlation with higher ratings.
- Conducted a **geographic analysis**, visualizing restaurant density and clustering.
- Studied **restaurant chains**, comparing their ratings and popularity.
- Performed a **sentiment analysis** on text reviews to extract common positive and negative keywords.
- Explored the correlation between **votes, ratings, and price ranges**.

---

## **Skills Demonstrated**

- **Data Analysis**: Extracting insights using Python and SQL.
- **Data Visualization**: Creating informative plots and maps using Matplotlib, Seaborn, and Plotly.
- **Statistical Analysis**: Investigating relationships and patterns in data.
- **Geospatial Analysis**: Mapping restaurant locations using latitude and longitude data.
- **Correlation Analysis**: Identifying relationships between variables (e.g., price vs. services).

---

## **Tools Used**

- **Python**: Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Plotly, Scikit-learn, NLTK.
- **Jupyter Notebook**: For documenting and presenting the analysis.

---

## **Outcome of the Project**

This project provided actionable insights into restaurant industry trends, consumer preferences, and service effectiveness. It also showcased the application of advanced analytics techniques to real-world datasets.
